Lky Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Lky! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with Lky on Discord.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Lky, we may collect the following information:

- User Messages: Lky may store messages sent in the server where the bot is active to perform certain functions.

- Discord User ID: Lky collects your Discord user ID to identify you and provide personalized responses.

- Server Information: Lky may store basic information about Discord servers for customized features.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

- Provide and improve the functionality of Lky Bot.

- Respond to user commands and inquiries.

- Track and analyze usage to improve performance.

4. Data Retention

We store the collected information only for as long as necessary to provide our services. We do not store user messages indefinitely. If you no longer want your data stored, you can request us to delete your data by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no data transmission method over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except where required by law or when necessary to provide our services. We may share data with trusted third-party services for hosting or technical support, but they are not allowed to use your data for any other purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data and request information about how it is used.

- Request the deletion of your data.

- Contact us with any privacy-related concerns or questions.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. Please check this page periodically for updates.
