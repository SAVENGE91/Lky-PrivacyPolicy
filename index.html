Introduction

Thank you for using Lky! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with Lky on Discord.

Information We Collect

User Messages

Lky may store messages sent in the server where the bot is active to perform certain functions.

Discord User ID

Lky collects your Discord user ID to identify you and provide personalized responses.

Server Information

Lky may store basic information about Discord servers for customized features.

How We Use Your Information

Functionality

Provide and improve the functionality of Lky Bot.

Response

Respond to user commands and inquiries.

Analytics

Track and analyze usage to improve performance.

Data Retention

We store the collected information only for as long as necessary to provide our services. We do not store user messages indefinitely. If you no longer want your data stored, you can request us to delete your data by contacting us.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no data transmission method over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.
